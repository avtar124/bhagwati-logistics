Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common logistics questions

How quickly can you provide a quote?

We typically provide quotes within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent requirements, call us directly for immediate assistance.

Do you provide GPS tracking?

Yes, all our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking. You'll receive real-time updates on your shipment's location and status.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Mumbai and Maharashtra, with expansion to other states. Contact us to check if we cover your specific route.

Do you handle emergency deliveries?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency logistics services. Call our emergency hotline for same-day and urgent deliveries.

What types of cargo do you transport?

We handle general cargo, heavy machinery, perishables, pharmaceuticals, and specialized goods with appropriate vehicles.

Is my cargo insured during transport?

Yes, we provide cargo insurance options. Transit insurance details will be included in your quote and service agreement.
